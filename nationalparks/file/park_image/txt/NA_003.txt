Cape Breton Highlands National Park is a Canadian national park on northern Cape Breton Island in Nova Scotia.It consists of mountains, valleys, waterfalls, rocky coastlines and the Cape Breton Highlands, a tundra-esque plateau. Forest types include Acadian and Boreal.
